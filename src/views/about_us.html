About Us

Established in 2016, Limay Senior High School has been committed to providing quality education that empowers students with the knowledge and skills they need to excel in their chosen careers. As an institution dedicated to academic excellence and holistic development, we offer a range of specialized programs designed to equip learners with real-world expertise. Through our dynamic curriculum, modern facilities, and passionate educators, we nurture future professionals, leaders, and innovators.

Located in Duale, Limay, Bataan, our school serves as a pillar of learning in the community, fostering a culture of excellence, discipline, and integrity. We strive to create an inclusive and supportive environment where students can grow intellectually and socially. With our commitment to innovation and continuous improvement, we aim to shape individuals who are not only prepared for higher education but also ready to contribute meaningfully to society.
