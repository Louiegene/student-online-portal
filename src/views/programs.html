Discover Our Specialized Courses

Explore our comprehensive curriculum designed to prepare students for academic excellence and limitless opportunities.

Academic Track

Science, Technology, Engineering & Mathematics (STEM)

Focuses on teaching students to solve problems, think critically, and be creative. STEM education also emphasizes project-based learning and practical skills.

Possible Careers: Engineer, Doctor, Scientist, Programmer, Mathematician, Architect, Environmental Specialist, Data Analyst, Researcher, Pharmacist

Accountancy, Business & Management (ABM)

Focuses on equipping students with foundational knowledge and skills in business management, financial concepts, corporate operations, and accounting.

Possible Careers: Accountant, Financial Analyst, Entrepreneur, Marketing Manager, Human Resources Manager, Business Consultant, Investment Banker, Economist, Operations Manager, Real Estate Agent

Humanities and Social Sciences (HUMSS)

Focuses on the study of human behavior, societal issues, culture, and the arts.

Possible Careers: Lawyer, Teacher, Psychologist, Journalist, Social Worker, Public Relations Specialist, Political Scientist, Historian, Sociologist, Foreign Service Officer

General Academic Strand (GAS)

Focuses on providing a broad, generalist approach to college preparation.

Possible Careers: College Professor, Civil Servant, Customer Service Representative, Sales Representative, Tourism Officer, Media Practitioner

Technical-Vocational-Livelihood (TVL) Track

Information & Communications Technology (ICT)

Training in computer programming, hardware, and digital skills.

  • Computer Programming I - Java
  • Computer Programming II - .NET Technologies
  • Computer Systems Servicing (CSS)

Home Economics (HE)

Training in culinary arts, hospitality management, and tourism.

  • Food and Beverages
  • Cookery
  • Bread and Pastry
  • Beauty Care (Nail Care)
  • Hairdressing
  • Wellness Massage
  • Dressmaking
  • Tailoring

Industrial Arts

Skills in welding, electrical installation, and repair.

  • Shielded Metal Arc Welding (SMAW)
  • Electrical Installation and Maintenance (EIM)
  • Electronic Products Assembly and Servicing (EPAS)
