Privacy Policy

Introduction

Limay Senior High School respects your right to privacy. We are committed to protecting your personal information in accordance with Republic Act No. 10173, otherwise known as the Data Privacy Act of 2012.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal information when you access and use the Limay Senior High School Enrollment and Student Portal ("Portal"). By using the Portal, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy.

Data Collection

The Portal collects personal information from students, parents, and guardians for enrollment, academic records management, and communication purposes. The information we collect includes but is not limited to:

  • Full Name
  • Date of Birth
  • Contact Number
  • Email Address
  • Sex
  • Guardian's Information
  • Academic Records

Some information is provided directly by the user during enrollment, while other data may be obtained from school records. Users who do not provide the required information may not be able to complete the enrollment process or access certain Portal features.

Data Usage

The collected personal information will be used for the following purposes:

  • Enrollment processing and verification
  • Academic records management
  • Communication of school-related announcements
  • Parent and student access to academic progress and documents
  • Compliance with government education requirements

Access and Correction

Users have the right to access, update, or request corrections to their personal information. Only authorized school personnel have access to collected data, and any requests for access must be formally submitted to the school’s administration.

Disclosure of Personal Information / Data Sharing

Personal information will only be disclosed in accordance with the law and for legitimate purposes, including:

  • Compliance with legal obligations (e.g., Department of Education reporting)
  • Coordination with parents and guardians regarding academic performance
  • Security and emergency purposes

Information Security

We implement security measures to protect personal information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, while we strive to ensure data security, users acknowledge that no online transmission is completely secure, and information may still be susceptible to external threats.

Data Breach Notification

In case of a data breach affecting users' personal information, we will take necessary steps to investigate, mitigate risks, and notify affected individuals if required by law.

Changes and Amendments

Limay Senior High School reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy as needed. Any changes will be communicated through the Portal or other official school channels. Continued use of the Portal after modifications constitute acceptance of the revised policy.
